UK Road Accidents analysis

This website conveys the results of the analysis of UK road accident data and is part of a project in the Social data and visualisation course at DTU. You can see some plain road accidents statistics, look into our machine learning models and predictions or explore the interactive D3 visualisations.

Also, if you want to know more, read our explainer notebook
